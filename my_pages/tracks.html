  • Advanced Machining Processes
  • Advanced Structural Materials
  • Advances in Computational Materials Science
  • Agile/lean/Intelligence Manufacturing
  • Alternate Materials/Material Subtitution
  • Application of Alloys/Composite techniques
  • Automation in Manufacturing/Robotics
  • Bio-mimetic & Bio-inspired Materials
  • Clean and Sustainable Manufacturing Processes
  • Composite, Intermatallics and Polymer Manufacturing
  • Computational mechanics and simulation
  • Computer-based manufacturing technologies
  • Design Tools, Cutting Tool Material
  • Failure and fracture mechanics
  • Flexible Manufacturing System
  • Functionality Graded Materials , Smart Materials
  • Graphene and 2D Materials
  • Green materials and Manufacturing
  • High-speed/Hybrid Machining Processes
  • Hydrometallurgy
  • Innovations in metal forming,bending & casting Techniques
  • Logistics and Supply Chain Management
  • Magnetic, Electronic and Optical Materials
  • MFMS Integration/Packaging
  • Metallurgy of Non-Ferrous, Rare and Precious metals
  • Metrology and computer aided inspection
  • Joining/Material Modifiaction Technology
  • Environmental Degradation of Materials
  • Surface Engineering
  • Structural Health Monitoring
  • Micro Machining
  • Nanomaterials and Nano-composites
  • Numeric Modelling and Simulation
  • Plastic Waste Management
  • Powder Metallurgy/Ceramic Forming
  • Precision Mechanics,Mechatronics
  • Quality & Reliability Engineering
  • Rapid manufacturing technologies and prototyping
  • Recycling and Re-manufacturing of Materials ans Components
  • Reverse Engineering and Concurrent Engineering
  • Sensors and Condition Monitoring
  • Thermal Spray
  • Thermally-Enhanced Processes and Materials
  • Thin and Thick Films
  • Total Quality Management and Quality Engineering
  • Ultra-Precision Machining
  • Virtual Manufacturing and Concurrent Engineering
  • Web-based Manufacturing

All submitted papers will be evaluated by the conference reviewer committee interms of quality , originality of approach and relevance to the theme of the conference . All accepted and registered papers will be published in SCOPUS INDEXED JOURNAL (it will be finalized soon) .
